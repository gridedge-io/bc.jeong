Optimal Secondary Control to Suppress Voltage Fluctuations in an HVDC-linked Wind Farm Grid
B. Jeong, D. Kwon, J. Park, Y. Kim, and O. Gomis-Bellmunt
IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, vol. 37, no. 4, pp. 2563–2577, July 2022
🏆 Selected as one of the 50 most frequently accessed documents in IEEE Transactions on Power Systems — July 2022
Proposes an optimal secondary control framework for suppressing voltage fluctuations in an HVDC-connected offshore wind farm grid. Develops a VSC-HVDC small-signal model integrating wind turbines, DFIGs, and bidirectional AC/DC converters, and designs an LQG controller for coordinated voltage control — validated against KEPCO's actual offshore grid configuration.

Implementation of Optimal Two-Stage Scheduling of Energy Storage System Based on Big-Data-Driven Forecasting — An Actual Case Study in a Campus Microgrid
B. Jeong, D. Shin, J. Im, J. Park, and Y. Kim
Energies, vol. 12, no. 6, pp. 1–20, March 2019
Implements a big-data-driven two-stage ESS scheduling framework using Mixed Integer Linear Programming, validated on an actual campus microgrid with PV and storage. Compared open-source solvers (CBC, GLPK) against commercial solvers (CPLEX, Gurobi) and demonstrated real-time operational feasibility — POSCO-funded and verified at POSTECH campus.
